about her.

Elara moved through the bustling market of Silverwood like a shadow, her hooded cloak concealing her face. Few noticed the intricate silver ring she wore, a royal crest from a kingdom she had abandoned years ago. She bartered for herbs and listened to rumors, always alert. Her past as Princess Aurelia haunted her, a life of gilded cages and political schemes she had fled to find freedom. But whispers of a growing darkness in her homeland tugged at her conscience, a call she couldn't ignore. The crown she had rejected might be the only thing that could save them all, but revealing her true identity would put her, and everyone she cared about, in mortal danger.
